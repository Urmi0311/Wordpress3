== Description ==

Retailer Market is a Wordpress theme with its layout giving professional websites for the marketplace, stores, shops, Woocommerce, grocery, multivendor shops, vegetable store, food delivery chains, food business, fashion stores, electronics, and any kind of online stores. This is a free theme with loads of features and its minimal impressive design can make your audience look directly at the important facts and key information published. It is clean and clutter-free making every product look exceptionally good on your website. Retina ready display will bring a clear view of images and responsive design make the layout adjust to every deviceâ€™s screen with perfection. With the user-friendly interface of this theme, it becomes easy for novices as well as beginners to get a wonderful design. Personalization options are going to make it simple for you to change the default colors and fonts. For better performance and faster page load times, it has well-optimized codes that will also make your website lightweight. SEO-friendly design can make your website have more visitors as it can get easily noticed. Call to Action Button (CTA) can make a huge difference in conversions and provide an interactive environment for your audience. With social media icons, you can have effective promotion options.


== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Retailer Market in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
